How to Play
Welcome to Guess The Word!
Game Objective:

Try to guess the secret 5-letter word in 5 attempts or less.

How to Play:
  1. Type any 5-letter word to start guessing
  2. Press 'Submit' or Enter key to submit your guess
  3. The colors will show how close your guess was
  4. Keep guessing until you find the word or run out of attempts
Color Guide:
Green: Letter is in the correct spot
Yellow: Letter is in the word but wrong spot
Gray: Letter is not in the word
Tips:
  • Use the colored feedback to make better guesses
  • Try to use common letters (E, A, R, I, O, T)
  • Remember, the same letter can appear multiple times
